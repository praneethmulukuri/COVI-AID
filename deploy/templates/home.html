Mission

The Corona Virus Disease also called the COVID-19, is a dangerous virus and declared as a pandemic by World Health Organization. It is a highly transmissible viral infection and has a severe impact on global health. More than 16 crore people across the world were affected and more than 33 lakh people died because of this virus. If the positive cases can be detected early, this pandemic disease spread can be restricted. Prediction of COVID-19 disease is advantageous to identify patients at risk of health conditions. Coronavirus shows its impact on many areas in the human body but primarily infects the respiratory system. COVID-19 causes pneumonia that tends to take hold in both the lungs. Pneumonia caused by the coronavirus is more serious than the normal pneumonia. This pneumonia can be found using a chest X-ray. Application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) techniques for COVID-19 prediction from X-rays can be very useful and can help to overcome the shortage of availability of doctors in remote places. Our mission is to propose a system that can be used for the automatic identification of the coronavirus from the chest X-ray images. In this method, the features of the chest X-ray images are extracted by using the Convolution neural networks(CNN), and then the extracted features are classified using the various classifiers. The concept of Transfer Learning can also be used for its advantage of decreasing the training time for a neural network model. This system enables us to detect the COVID-19 infected patients in a better and more accurate way.
